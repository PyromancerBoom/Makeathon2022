Who are we?

Pride is a celebration of the LGBTQ+ community and so are we! As cliche as it will sound, our team believes in making the world a better place for each and every person out there, no matter their gender identities.


If you are a part of the mighty rainbow, you are a part of us. We at the Proud Place provide people a platform for people to embark on their journey of self discovery and acceptance . Our aim is to serve as a safe haven for every queer artist, writer and creative director.
